Hearth & Hollow
Two-player asymmetric coop. One of you is the lumbering stone Golem (mouse) — strong, slow, walks the misty forest. The other is the tiny Fire Sprite in its chest (WASD + E) — quick, smart, lights the runes that give the Golem its powers.
Wolves charge, mist blinds, gaps yawn. The sprite's runes pulse red when the right one is needed next — call it out.
